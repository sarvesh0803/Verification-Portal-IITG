Introduction

The verification portal has been made for one purpose: to get all your achievements and participation in any actitivity in your college life easily verified through a secure channel. Your profile URL in this portal is public; can be shared and put on your resume.

To get your posts or achievements verified, you first need to search for the team you wish to apply in. Click apply, fill in your post name and hit submit. That's it!

Layout of the portal

The portal has been divided into five tabs (only see four?), each of which is accessible from the sidebar to your leftby clicking the hamburger menu on the top right of the page. The logout button is on the bottom of the sidebar. Clicking on the tab name takes you to it's page. Each page has been explained below.

Though the portal is responsive, we recommend using it on screens greater than 992px wide (read laptops or larger).

Dashboard

The dashboard is the page you should see every time you login and is where you can check the status of all your applications. It has been divided into four sections (only see three?) accessible from the navigation bar on top.

Search

The search page is where you find teams and apply in them. You should see a familiar search bar with a handy quick instructions panel.

Create

This page is basically a form which can be used to create new teams.

This page has restricted access and only people with administrative rights can access it.

Profile

The profile page shows all your approved posts. Generate PDF.

This page is public and the URL can be shared.

Searching for a team

Click on the Search option on the sidebar. Type your keyword in the search bar and the results should immediately pop up. You can search by Team name or it's subtitle. Each search result will have the team name, subtitle, logo, year and team creator to help you find the right team.

Can't find your team?

Applying for a post

After having found the team you want to apply in, click on Apply at the bottom right of the card. You should see a popup with the team details and an input field. Type in the post name/achievement you want to apply for and click on Submit. You can now track your application in the Dashboard.

For now we are only permitting applying in a team of the current academic year.

  • Double check the team details you are applying in.
  • Do not apply for multiple posts in one request. Make a new request for each post.
  • Make sure the post name is properly formatted as this will be the official proof for every achievement.

Creating a team

Definition of a team: Any planned event or any annual team of any body/club/occasion.

Click on the Create button (don't see the button?) on the sidebar. The create page is a form. To create a new team you have to fill the form with your team details. The team name (e.g. Alcheringa), subtitle (e.g. The Annual Cultural Festival), year (e.g. 2017-2018), under the aegis of (e.g. Cultural Board) are the four fields to be filled.

After submitting the team details, your request can be tracked in the Dashboard.

There will be multiple teams of the same body. For example, there will be multiple ‘teams’ of ‘Cepstrum, The Student Body of EEE’ distinguished by their academic year of operation ‘under the aegis’ of ‘Departmental Body: Cepstrum’. This will allow for creation of annual teams of fixed bodies which work round the year. Similarly, there will be multiple ‘teams’ of ‘Octaves, The Music Club’ distinguished by their year ‘under the aegis’ of ‘Cultural Board’.

Tracking your applications

All your applications (team or post) can be tracked in the Dashboard. Each application card will have the team name, post name, team creator, team year, and body.

The pending tab shows all your requests that are in progress. Hovering over the progress bar shows more details.
The approved tab shows all your posts and teams that been approved. Teams and posts will be split into two sections.
The declined tab shows all your requests that have been declined with the person who declined your request and the reason for declination.

The request tab is where you can approve or decline the requests that have come to you. You cannot decline a request without giving a reason.

Body: Mention the current name and roll number you have in the portal alongwith the changes you want.

Attachment: A photo of your IITG ID card with name and roll number clearly visible.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • I can't find my team. What should I do?

    That is probably because it has not been made yet. Contact your secretary and ask him to create one.

  • Why can't I create a team?

    Some rights in the portal have been reserved for people with positions of responsibility. You can contact them to create a team if need be.

  • My request is not moving past the first stage. What should I do?

    Please check whether the team you applied in is of the current academic year. For now, we are only permitting requests of the current academic year.

  • My request is of the current year and is still not showing any progress. What should I do?

    Hover over the progress bar to know where the request is pending. You can then contact the concerned person.
